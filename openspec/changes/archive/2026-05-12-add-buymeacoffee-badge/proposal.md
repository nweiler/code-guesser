## Why

Buy Me a Coffee is the standard way open-source projects accept support. Adding a badge gives the community a path to show appreciation and signals this project is open to support. Low effort, high convention.

## What Changes

- Add a Buy Me a Coffee badge/link in the page footer, alongside "View on GitHub" and "Suggest a repo"
- Configure the Buy Me a Coffee username/URL so it's easy to change

## Capabilities

### New Capabilities
- `donation-support`: Link to an external donation/support page (Buy Me a Coffee), displayed as a branded badge in the site footer

## Impact

- Game.tsx: footer section updated with the new badge
- No new dependencies — Buy Me a Coffee badge loads from their CDN or is a plain link with their button styling
